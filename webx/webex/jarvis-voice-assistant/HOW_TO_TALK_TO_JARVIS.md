# How to Talk to Jarvis - Quick Guide

## 🎤 **Option 1: SPEAK to Jarvis (RECOMMENDED)**

**Just Double-Click This File:**
```
CLICK_TO_SPEAK_TO_JARVIS.bat
```

### What Happens:
1. A window opens with Jarvis ready
2. Jarvis says: "Hello! I'm Jarvis. Press Enter and speak to me!"
3. **Press ENTER** (don't type anything)
4. **Speak for 5 seconds** into your microphone
5. Jarvis transcribes, thinks, and responds with voice!

### Example Conversation:
```
Press ENTER to speak: [ENTER]
[LISTENING] Recording for 5 seconds...
>> Speak clearly into your microphone now!

[You said]: "Tell me a joke"
[Jarvis is thinking...]
Jarvis: "Why don't scientists trust atoms? Because they make up everything!"
[Speaking...]
```

## 🔧 **Requirements:**

- ✅ Working microphone (for voice input)
- ✅ Working speakers (to hear Jarvis)
- ✅ API keys already configured in `configs/voice_config.yaml`

---

## 🎯 **Commands While Talking to Jarvis:**

- **Press ENTER** = Record your voice
- **Type a message** = Text input (Jarvis still speaks response)
- **Type 'quit'** = Exit

---

## 💡 **Tips:**

1. **Speak clearly** and at normal volume
2. **Wait for the prompt** before speaking
3. **Say complete sentences** (works better than fragments)
4. **If recording fails**, just type your message instead
